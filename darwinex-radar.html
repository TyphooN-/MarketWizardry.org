📋 What Darwinex RADAR Tracks

  • 🆕 New Symbol Listings: Chronological tracking of newly added instruments
  • ❌ Delisted Symbols: Historical record of removed instruments
  • 📊 Symbol Count Changes: Net change statistics over time
  • 📅 Date Range Analysis: Trading day coverage and historical depth
  • 🔍 Market Evolution: Long-term market composition trends

🎯 Why Use Darwinex RADAR?

  • Market Coverage Awareness: Stay informed about Darwinex's expanding instrument selection
  • Trading Opportunities: Identify newly available instruments for trading strategies
  • Risk Management: Track which instruments may be discontinued
  • Historical Context: Understand market evolution and broker coverage changes
  • Data Validation: Verify symbol availability for backtesting and analysis

💻 Automated Generation

Darwinex RADAR reports are automatically generated daily:

python3 update_all_tools.py

# Generate RADAR reports only
python3 darwinex-radar/symbol_tracker.py --all
