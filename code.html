Open Source Code

MQL5 NNFX Risk Management System - Magic Key inspired Risk Managment EA + NNFX style trading system (full indicators included). VaR based risk management supported.

MQL5 ExAmples - Example EAs/functions that do not make sense to exist in other repos.

MQL5 MalciousEAmporium - Proof of Concept EAs (Expert Advisor) to show the potential dangers of running a random EA on a trading account.

Darwin Research - Python and R scripts to analyze raw DARWIN data.

Trading Strategies - Various trading algorithms and strategies.
